2. Policy Scope

This policy applies to data collected when visitors use the website, contact us, sign up for newsletters, become prospects or clients, or use our services. It does not cover third-party websites.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use four categories of cookies: Essential (security and forms), Performance/Analytics (e.g. Google Analytics), Functional (preference retention), and Advertising (only with opt-in). You can manage cookie settings via your browser.

7. Third-Party Sharing

Data may be shared with cloud hosting providers, payment processors, analytics platforms, legal and accounting advisors, and project subcontractors. All vendors must maintain confidentiality. We do not sell data. Legal disclosure only occurs if court-ordered.

8. Data Retention

We use encryption, access controls, security reviews, and staff training to protect your data. We will notify you of any breach as legally required.

10. International Transfers

Systems may transfer data internationally. Standard contractual clauses and approved frameworks ensure protection.
