The Curriculum

8 Modules. Zero Fluff.

Every module is built from real experience running and scaling a 6-figure agency — not theory.

01 Skill & Niche Selection
Find your profitable niche and define your service offering. Identify high-demand skills, evaluate market opportunities, and position yourself to attract premium clients from day one.
02 Team Building & Operations
Build your team with the right structure and revenue share model. Learn how to recruit, onboard, and manage talent so you can scale without burning out.
03 Digital Presence
Website, social profiles, and credibility online. Build a digital footprint that generates inbound leads and positions you as an authority in your niche.
04 Paid Advertising
Meta & Google ad campaigns that actually convert. From campaign setup to creative strategy to optimization — learn to generate qualified leads predictably.
05 Personal Brand
Content strategy, scripting, and building authority. Learn to create content that attracts ideal clients and establishes trust before the first conversation.
06 Cold Outreach
LinkedIn, email sequences, and landing meetings. Proven outreach templates, follow-up cadences, and personalization strategies that get responses.
07 Cold Calling
Scripts, objection handling, and phone sales mastery. Word-for-word scripts, live call breakdowns, and proven frameworks for booking meetings.
08 In-Person Sales
Door-to-door, events, and field sales playbook. Face-to-face selling — from networking events to walking into businesses and closing on the spot.
