The Curriculum

8 Modules. Zero Fluff.

01 Skill & Niche Selection
Find your profitable niche and define your service offering. You'll identify high-demand skills, evaluate market opportunities, and position yourself to attract premium clients from day one.
02 Team Building & Operations
Build your team with the right structure and revenue share model. Learn how to recruit, onboard, and manage talent so you can scale without burning out.
03 Digital Presence
Website, social profiles, and establishing credibility online. Build a digital footprint that generates inbound leads and positions you as an authority in your niche.
04 Paid Advertising
Meta & Google ad campaigns that actually convert. From campaign setup to creative strategy to optimization — learn to generate qualified leads predictably.
05 Personal Brand
Content strategy, scripting, and building authority. Learn how to create content that attracts your ideal clients and establishes trust before the first conversation.
06 Cold Outreach
LinkedIn, email sequences, and landing meetings. Get proven outreach templates, follow-up cadences, and personalization strategies that get responses.
07 Cold Calling
Scripts, objection handling, and phone sales mastery. Master the phone with word-for-word scripts, live call breakdowns, and proven frameworks for booking meetings.
08 In-Person Sales
Door-to-door, events, and field sales playbook. Learn the art of face-to-face selling — from networking events to walking into businesses and closing on the spot.

Who is this for?
This is for freelancers, side-hustlers, and aspiring agency owners who want to build a real digital consulting business. Whether you do web design, marketing, SEO, or any digital service — if you want to go from solo to agency, this is for you.
How much does it cost?
Pricing is shared during the application process. We keep enrollment limited to ensure quality, so pricing reflects the level of access and mentorship included. Apply to learn more about current pricing and payment options.
How long do I have access?
You get lifetime access to the course content and community. All future module updates and additions are included at no extra cost. The live coaching calls run on an ongoing basis as long as you're an active member.
What if I'm a complete beginner?
That's exactly who Module 1 is built for. We start from scratch — identifying your skills, picking a niche, and building your first offer. Many of our most successful students started with zero experience in running an agency.

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you go through the material, attend the calls, and genuinely feel it's not for you, we'll work with you on a refund. We're confident in the value — but we want you to feel confident too.
